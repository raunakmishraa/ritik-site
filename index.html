Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

CMAT stands for Central Management Aptitude Test which is an entrance exam conducted annually by the Faculty of Management, Tribhuvan University, generally in the month of August / September after the announcement of the Grade XII result.

It is necessary to give CMAT exam in order to study top Management Courses listed below in colleges affiliated to TU. The courses that requires mandatory CMAT results are:

  • Bachelor in Travel and Tourism Management (BTTM)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Business Management (BBM)
  • Bachelor of Hotel Management (BHM)
  • Bachelor of Information Management (BIM)
  • Bachelor of Mountaineering Studies (BMS)
  • Bachelor of Public Administration (BPA)

  • Shankar Dev Campus (SDC)
  • Nepal Commerece Campus (NCC)
  • Nepal Academy of Tourism and Hotel Management (NATHM) - For BHM/BTTM
  • Padma Kanya Campus (PK)
  • Madan Bhandari Memorial College (MMMC)
  • Public Youth Campus
  • Saraswati Multiple Campus
  • Bhaktapur Multiple Campus
  • Patan Multiple Campus
